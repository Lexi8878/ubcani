March 4, 2023

Pop-Up Hanami!

It’s time to finally unveil our biggest event
of the year: Pop-Up Hanami! Inspired by the sakura
trees scattered around UBC Campus and the Japanese
tradition of Hanami (花見) or “Flower Viewing”, UBCAni
will be pulling out all the stops this year to create the
largest and most complete event in our clubs history!

The event will include:

  • A Sakura Cosplay Cafe, where Servers in full
    cosplay will be bringing you some of the best pastries
    and desserts Vancouver has to offer (at a reasonable price),
    and handmade anime-inspired drinks.
  • An Artist Alley, where you can purchase exclusive
    and never-before-seen art made by our club’s most talented
    artists, and other vendors including some of our amazing sponsors!
  • Performances including idol groups, DJ sets, and non-stop
    J-pop, J-rock, and anime music for the entire event!
  • And tons of games (including a Pop-Up Hanami scavenger hunt)
    where you can earn tickets to increase your chances of winning
    one of our many prizes!

For more info, click the “Pop-Up Hanami” tab on the website above!

February 3, 2023

UBCAni Valentine’s Social:
The Way of the House Weeb!

Get ready to level up your housewarming skills and become the
ultimate houseweeb at UBCAni’s 2023 Valentines Social event! UBC Ani
is proud to announce our “The Way of the Househusband” themed
Valentine’s event, which will take place onSaturday, February 11th!

Doors will open at 4:30 PM and the event will start at 5:00 PM sharp!
This time, UBC Ani invites you to join us in the Great Hall North in the
UBC Nest, 2nd Floor, where you can make bentos for your wife with one hand,
while defending your territory from yakuza with the other!! We’re excited to
be able to host a social in a bigger venue, so more people can enjoy the event.

Event Schedule:

4:30PM : Doors open
5:00PM : Games and activities
7:00PM : Refreshments Break
7:15PM : UAM performance!
8:00PM : Cosplay Contest (signup here!)
8:15PM : Raffle Draw
8:30PM : Dance Party and Mini-Karaoke
9:00PM : End of the event!
