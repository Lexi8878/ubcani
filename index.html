Welcome to UBC Ani!

The UBC Anime Club is a student-run organization that brings
together individuals who share a passion for anime, manga,
and Japanese culture. We have members from all across the
lower mainland, and we host big and small events all
throughout the year.

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What does the club do?
A: We are a social club that meets on and off campus at UBC! We
host weekly meetups every Friday on campus, featuring anime
viewings, artist meetups, and a different main activity each week!

Q: How much are memberships?
Annual memberships are $10.00 for UBC Students, and $15.00
for external members. Have a membership card from previous years?
Returning members are eligible for a discount!

Q: How do I purchase a membership?
A:Memberships are available for purchase after September Clubs
Fair each year. You can buy memberships at every one of our events
following Clubs Fair, or you can schedule an appointment with a UBCAni
Executive! Feel free to DM us or send us an email at info@ubcani.com

Q: What are membership perks?
A: Some perks include:

  • Discounts of 10-20% at our sponsor locations across the lower mainland
  • Bonus raffle tickets and discounted entry to special events
  • A shiny new membership card design each year!

Q: I have more questions!
